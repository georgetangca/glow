Diabetic eye disease is a complication of diabetes. Sometimes there are symptoms, such as a blurred or spotty vision. But it can also be symptomless, which is why regular eye exams are so important. Maintaining healthy levels of blood sugar is important for prevention of diabetic retinopathy, which could lead to vision loss.
