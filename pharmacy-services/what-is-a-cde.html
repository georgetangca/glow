CDE is an acronym for Certified Diabetes Educator.

What is a CDE?

Some people in the healthcare industry may become specialized to help patients with diabetes. They can help manage your diabetes by reviewing the following with you:

  • General diabetes information
  • Diabetes medication information
  • Blood glucose testing—selection of appropriate blood glucose meter, importance of checking blood glucose, how often and when to check your blood glucose, interpreting your results
  • Treatment of hypoglycemia (low blood sugar) and hyperglycemia (high blood sugar)
  • Recommendations for healthy eating
  • Recommendations for physical activity
  • Foot-care recommendations
  • Other resources for diabetes support
